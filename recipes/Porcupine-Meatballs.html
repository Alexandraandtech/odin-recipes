Lets Get Started. Ingredients Needed

  • 1/2 Pound Ground Beef (80/20)
  • 1/2 Pound Ground Sausage or ground pork, lamb, veal
  • 1/3 Cup Jasmine Rice
  • 1 Campbell Tomato Soup Can
  • Worstechire Sauce
  • Johnny's Seasoning
  • Water
  1. Mix ground beef and sausage and add jasmine rice, worstechire and Johnny's seasoning to taste. Mix well.
  2. Form into balls and set aside.
  3. In a large pan add can of tomato soup and 3/4 of water.
  4. Add a few dashes of worstechire and mix then turn stove to medium heat.
  5. Add Meatballs and cover. Check and add seasoning to taste.
