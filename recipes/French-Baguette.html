Lets Get Started! French Baguette Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 Cups All Purpose Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • 1 Tablespoon Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 to 1 1/2 Cups of Warm Water
  • 1 Packet of Active Dry Yeast
  1. Mix all dry Ingredients (flour, sugar, salt, active dry yeast)
  2. Add 1 cup of warm water slowly and mix. Note: If mixture requires more water add rest of water
  3. Kneed dough by hand or in Kitchen Aid until smooth.
  4. In a bowl add olive oil and add dough and cover it for approx. 15-30 minutes or until its doubled in size.
  5. Once dough has doubled in size cut in two and roll. You can cut the with a knife. Cover rolls and allow to rise for another 15-30 minutes or until doubled in size.
  6. Pre heat oven to 375 degrees add dough for 15 minutes
